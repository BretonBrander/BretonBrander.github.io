Cutie Duty Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

Cutie Duty does not operate developer-run accounts, analytics, advertising, or tracking. Most relationship content stays local on your device. If you choose to send partner affirmations through the app, those affirmations use Apple's iCloud and CloudKit sharing so the people you invite can receive them.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store saved affirmations, draft affirmation text, reminder settings, question progress, and conversation state locally on your device using iOS local storage.

Partner Affirmation Sharing

If you invite a partner and send an affirmation through the app, the affirmation text, selected trait labels, sender display name, and sent date are stored using Apple's CloudKit service in iCloud. The affirmation is shared with the people you invite into that shared iCloud space.

You can avoid this by not inviting a partner and by using local drafts or the native iOS share sheet instead.

Apple Intelligence And On-Device Generation

Affirmation drafting may use Apple on-device language model features when they are available on your device. If they are not available, the app uses a local fallback generator.

The app does not send affirmation prompts to a third-party AI service.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app uses iOS local notifications. Reminder schedules are stored locally on your device. Partner affirmation sharing may use Apple push notification infrastructure through CloudKit so the app can refresh shared affirmations.

Data We Do Not Collect

This version does not include developer-operated accounts, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking across apps or websites, or sale of personal information.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact form.
