Cutie Duty Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

Cutie Duty is designed to keep relationship work private and local whenever possible.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store saved affirmations, draft affirmation text, reminder settings, question progress, and conversation state locally on your device using iOS local storage.

We do not operate a backend for this version of the app.

Apple Intelligence And On-Device Generation

Affirmation drafting may use Apple on-device language model features when they are available on your device. If they are not available, the app uses a local fallback generator.

The app does not send affirmation prompts to a third-party AI service.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app uses iOS local notifications. Reminder schedules are stored locally on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

This version does not include developer-operated accounts, developer-operated cloud sync, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking across apps or websites, or sale of personal information.
