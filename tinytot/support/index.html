Support

We can help.

For support, use the Brander Design contact form.

For help with Tiny Tot, feature requests, bug reports, or account and iCloud sharing questions, send a message through the contact form and include the details below when relevant.

Helpful details to include

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version.
  • What you were trying to log or change.
  • Whether the issue involves Siri, reminders, widgets, iCloud sharing, or Live Activities.
  • Any error message shown in the app.

iCloud sharing

Tiny Tot uses Apple iCloud sharing for caregiver collaboration. If an invite link does not open correctly, make sure the receiving iPhone is signed in to iCloud, has iCloud Drive enabled, and is using the latest available app version.
