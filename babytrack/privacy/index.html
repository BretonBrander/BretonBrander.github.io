BabyTrack Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

BabyTrack is designed to help caregivers track baby care events such as bottles, food, diapers, sleep, reminders, and shared caregiver activity.

Data stored by the app

The app stores baby profile details, care logs, reminder settings, caregiver names, notes you enter, and app preferences on your device. If you enable iCloud sharing, Apple iCloud and CloudKit store and sync this data so invited caregivers can access the shared baby care space.

Data we collect

BabyTrack does not use advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or third-party trackers. The app does not operate a separate developer server for baby logs.

If you contact support, we may receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, screenshots, device details, or a description of the issue.

Apple services

Some features use Apple-provided services on your device or Apple account, including iCloud, Siri, Shortcuts, widgets, Live Activities, and local notifications. Those services are governed by Apple’s terms and privacy policies.

Caregiver sharing

When you invite caregivers, they may be able to view and update shared baby care information through Apple iCloud sharing. You control who is invited through Apple’s sharing controls.

Deleting data

You can delete local app data by deleting the app from your device. If you use iCloud sharing, you may also need to remove participants or delete the shared iCloud space from the app’s sharing controls.

Children’s privacy

BabyTrack is intended for parents and caregivers, not for use by children. The app is not directed to children as its primary users.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the BabyTrack support page.
